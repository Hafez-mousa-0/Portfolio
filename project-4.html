Process Review & Deployment

Project Overview

Redesigned an incomplete workflow process by implementing review stages and deployment changes. The project focused on improving process efficiency, adding quality control checkpoints, and streamlining the deployment workflow.

Transformation Process

The process improvement transformed from an old design (Before) into a comprehensive modern system with review and deployment stages (After):

Note: Images on the left are "Before" and images on the right are "After".

Key Improvements

  • Added review stage to ensure quality before deployment
  • Implemented clear approval workflow with multiple checkpoints
  • Streamlined deployment process with automated steps
  • Enhanced visibility into process status and progress
  • Reduced errors and improved overall process reliability
  • Better tracking and documentation of changes

Design Decisions

The transformation from an incomplete process to a comprehensive workflow with review and deployment stages significantly improved process reliability and quality. By adding clear checkpoints and structured stages, we reduced errors and improved team collaboration.
